Our Story

Built on Reliability,
Driven by Craft

We started as a one-truck operation in Hollywood, FL with a simple promise: show up on time, fix it right, and charge a fair price.

Over the past decade, that promise has earned us the trust of thousands of homeowners, property managers, and commercial businesses across Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach counties.

Today, Mega Garage Door & Gates is South Florida's go-to team for garage door repair, electric gate installation, motor service, intercom systems, fencing, and custom metalwork — all under one roof, with licensed and insured technicians on call around the clock.

We don't subcontract. Every job is handled by our own trained team, using quality parts and standing behind every installation with a warranty.
